Welcome to the Annual Artichoke Festival 2023!

"Life is like eating artichokes; you have got to go through so much to get so little." ~ Tad Dorgan

Love eating large thistlelike perennial plants? Join us May 10th at the Thistle Arena

Our Mission

To raise money to give back to the surrounding communities of North Idaho, in a wonderful celebration of the artichoke.

Our Purpose

To engage, inform and excite festival attendees about thse unique qualities of artichokes including farming, harvest, preparation, and eating and to demonstrate how the artichoke industry has influenced the history of North Idaho

Schedule

  • 10am: Festival opens
  • 11am-12pm: Cooking demonstration by Chef John
  • 12pm-1pm: Artichoke tasting featuring different artichoke dishes from local restaurants
  • 1pm-2pm: Live music by the band Artichoke Hearts
  • 2pm-3pm: Artichoke-themed craft workshop where you can learn how to make artichoke centerpieces
  • 3pm-4pm: Cooking demonstration by Chef Maria
  • 4pm-5pm: Artichoke tasting featuring different artichoke dishes from local restaurants
  • 5pm: Festival closes

Guest Speakers and Preformers

Artichoke Hearts

Artichoke Hearts is a four-piece indie rock band from Brooklyn, New York. The band's music is characterized by catchy guitar riffs, driving drum beats, and introspective lyrics that explore themes of love, loss, and self-discovery. The band's lead singer, Emily, has a powerful voice that can go from a whisper to a scream in seconds. The other members of the band are Jake on guitar, Max on bass, and Sarah on drums. Artichoke Hearts has released two albums and has toured extensively throughout the United States and Europe.

Archibald "Artie" Chokestein

He was born into a family of artichoke farmers and always had a passion for cooking with the vegetable. He eventually became a celebrity chef, known for his artichoke-based dishes, and even opened up his own restaurant called "Chokestein's."

Choka Zilla

Choke-a-zilla was once a small and unassuming artichoke farmer, until one day he was exposed to a strange chemical that caused him to mutate into a giant artichoke monster. At first, Choke-a-zilla was scared of his new form and hid away from the world. But eventually, he learned to embrace his powers and use them for good. He now spends his days protecting artichoke farms from harm and using his strength to help those in need. Despite his fearsome appearance, Choke-a-zilla has a heart of gold and is beloved by all who know him.
